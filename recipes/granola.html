Healthy Granola

Yummy, healthy granola that's quick to make

This granola recipe, originally sourced from Gimme Some Oven, is a quick and easy recipe that tastes great, without a lot of sweetner.

Ingredients
  • 4 cups old fashioned oats
  • 1 cup pecans, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened coconut flakes or 1/2 cup shredded
  • 1/2 cup dried fruit
Instructions
  1. Heat oven to 180C. Line large baking sheet with baking paper and set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, stir together oats, pecans, chia seeds, cinnamon, and sea salt until evenly combined
  3. In a separate measuring cup, stir together the coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until combined. Pour wet mixture into the oat mixture, and stir until combined
  4. Spread the granola out evenly on the baking sheet. Bake for 20 minutes, stirring once halfway through. Remove from oven and add the coconut, then bake for 5 more minutes, until the coconut is lightly toasted and golden.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool. Then stir in dried fruit or any other optional add-ins you might prefer.
  6. Store in airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 month.
