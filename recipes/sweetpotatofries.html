Spicy Sweet Potato Baked Fries

This recipe is easy to double-up if you're serving more than two people.

Ingredients
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced into 1cm (½in) thick fries
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt pinch of ground cinnamon
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C
  2. In a large bowl combine the sweet potato fries and sunflower oil.
  3. In a smaller bowl, mix together the smoked paprika, garlic powder, sea salt and cinnamon. Toss this through the sweet potato until all the fries are evenly coated.
  4. Spread out evenly over two baking trays, making sure that none of the fries are overlapping - this will ensure that they cook evenly and become crisp. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes until golden brown and crisp at the edges. Serve hot.

The spice mix can be mixed and stored in an airtight jar for up to 4 weeks - why not use it to spice up jacket potatoes?
